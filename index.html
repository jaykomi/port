Kakeibo

30-Day Kakeibo was my first mobile Android application. The main focus of the application was to help its users become more aware of their spending habits by enabling them to create a household ledger on their mobile Android devices.

The user would register their income and spending habits for 30 days. After these 30 days, they would review their performance, automatically setting a new goal for the next 30-day period.

02.

Let's Play!

Let's Play has by far been my favourite project up until now. The main goal of the application was to develop software in combination with hardware, that would allow children who suffer from mental disabilities to come in contact with games that required them to be physically active.

The application made use of RFID tags to identify and register health workers as well as children allowing them to collect game data and connect it to a person.

The game consisted of a quiz in which the button on individual Arduino's would match either a right or wrong answer. Through the use of RF-24 modules, the buttons could be placed up to a 100 meters apart creating the incetive to move.
